# NieR:Automata — PIXEL CINEMA · ROUTE A + ENDING E
### Same story. Different grammar. ~4:40 + hold, letterboxed 2.39:1.
**Music:** the Piano Collections "Weight of the World" MIDI — the score.
**Rule:** no "game view." Every scene is a composed shot or a cut between
shots: wides, silhouettes, close-ups, slow pans. Subtitles are in-world
dialogue only, set in the lower letterbox bar like theatrical subs.

## Sequences

### 1 · COLD OPEN (18s)
Black. The monologue types out — and beneath it, slowly, a face fades in:
2B in close-up, blindfolded, filling the frame. She breathes. That's all.

### 2 · DESCENT (16s)
EXTREME WIDE: a wall of dawn cloud, tiny flight units streaking down,
flak blooming soft and silent. CUT — COCKPIT CU: her visor, cloudlight
sliding across it. "This is YoRHa unit 2B. Commencing descent."

### 3 · THE FACTORY (24s)
LOW ANGLE, ANKLE HEIGHT: Marx fills the sky, furnace-backlit; 2B stands
tiny in the foreground, blade down. CUT — CU: her gloved hand tightening
on the hilt. CUT — EXTERIOR, FAR: the factory on the horizon; a white
bloom swells out of it, slow and silent. "Glory to Mankind."

### 4 · THE BUNKER (14s)
GOD SHOT: Earth, enormous; the Bunker a sliver crossing its face.
CUT — INTERIOR: 2B at the long window, back to camera, earthlight on her.

### 5 · CITY RUINS (26s)
ONE SHOT: a slow lateral pan across a vast painted ruinscape — towers,
green, birds — while two small figures walk. The Weight of the World walk,
as pure cinema this time.

### 6 · THE DESERT (24s)
WIDE INTO THE SUN: mask machines kneel in silhouette against a huge disc
of light. LOW ANGLE: the pile stirs; a golden figure stands up out of it,
backlit. CU: Adam's new face. His eyes open.

### 7 · AMUSEMENT PARK (20s)
WIDE: the dead ferris wheel against violet night, confetti drifting like
snow. AUDIENCE POV: Simone on stage, tiny 2B crossing the beam light.

### 8 · FOREST KINGDOM (16s)
The cradle in a single god ray. ONE FRAME of white slash-smear.
Aftermath: the ray, the empty cradle, petals of light. Nobody speaks.

### 9 · FLOODED CITY (18s)
SYMMETRY: drowned towers mirrored perfectly in still water. CUT — descent
through blue: light shafts, bubbles, two sinking silhouettes.

### 10 · EVE (26s)
RIDGE DUEL: two silhouettes trading blows against a burning sky. CU: 2B's
face in the red light — and one tear escaping below the blindfold.
OVER-SHOULDER: she holds 9S. "It always... ends like this."

### 11 · ENDING A (6s) → ENDING E (30s)
The card. Then the credits — rendered as a distant battle of lights seen
through falling names, the pods carrying the dialogue.
"Do you wish for them to live?" — "...Yes. Everything."

### 12 · DAWN (hold)
WIDE: the grass field. SLOW CUT to the last close-up of the film:
2B's face at rest — and for the first time, no blindfold. Eyes closed.
Dawn on her face. — fin —
