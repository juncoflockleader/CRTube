# NIER REPLICANT — 16-BIT · KAINÉ
### A pixel-art short film. One HTML file. ~104 seconds + hold.
**Music:** the user's "Kainé / Escape" MIDI (two-hand piano, steady 127.5 BPM, ~4:04),
embedded and rendered through the chiptune engine — track-aware voicing (left hand →
triangle, right hand → square lead). Because the tempo is constant, a chiptune drum
grid locks onto the beat — but **only** in the two battle scenes. The film runs at the
music's pace: this one moves.

> They called her monster.

---

## Palette
Automata was ash. Spira was water. The Aerie is **vertigo**: cold cliff greys and
violet chasm dark, shade-gold eyes, one warm porch sunset, and at the end a field of
white lunar tears. Kainé herself is the brightest thing in every frame — white hair,
white clothes, white bandages down her left side.

## Scenes

### 0 · TITLE — *silence*
A chasm at night, wooden walkways, a pale moon.
**NIER REPLICANT — 16-BIT · KAINÉ** · *PRESS START*

### 1 · THE AERIE (16s) — *music enters quietly*
A village strung across a gorge on planks and rope. Hooded villagers stand in
doorways; as the camera holds, the doors close one by one. A small girl stands
alone at the far end of a walkway.
> *They called her monster. This is her story —*
> *and she would hate every word of it.*

### 2 · GRANDMOTHER (16s) — *verse*
A porch on the cliff edge at sunset — the one warm frame in the film. Kainé and
Kali sit side by side, feet over the drop.
> KALI: "You are my treasure. Live, Kainé."
In the last seconds the sunset goes dark — and something vast and lizard-like
hauls itself out of the chasm, jaw first, one eye glowing like a lantern.
The giant Shade. The one that takes her grandmother.

### 3 · TYRANN (14s) — *lowpass, dropped notes, detune — the music sickens*
Black. Kainé lies broken. Violet shade-static crawls up her left side; a huge
pair of eyes opens above her.
> TYRANN: "WANNA LIVE, GIRL?"
> KAINÉ: "I won't die. Not yet."
White bandages wrap the corruption, one band at a time.

### 4 · THE HUNT (20s) — *drums lock in; full drive*
Side-scroll at speed: Kainé sprints with twin blades, cutting through shades —
black blobs with lantern eyes that burst into violet motes. Nier runs behind her,
greatsword out; Emil floats above, raining gold sparks.
> WEISS: "Such language, madam Kainé!"

### 5 · PETRIFIED (16s) — *hushed, half-speed feel*
The Aerie again. Kainé kneels; grey stone climbs her row by pixel row until she
is a statue on the walkway. Nier stands before her, fists clenched.
> KAINÉ: "Come back for me."
> `FIVE YEARS PASS.` — the moon arcs overhead, the village lights go out.

### 6 · ESCAPE (22s) — *drums return, lead doubled an octave up — peak*
Cracks spider across the stone. It bursts (soft petals, no flash). Kainé runs —
full sprint across collapsing walkways, planks dropping into the chasm behind
her heels, shades lunging and dying mid-stride.
> "RUN."
> KAINÉ: "OUT OF MY WAY."

### 7 · LUNAR TEARS (hold) — *the piano keeps going, far away now*
Dawn. A field of white flowers glowing faintly, swaying. Kainé stands still in
the middle of it, bandages loose in the wind.
> *Someone is calling her name.*
`— SHE LIVES —` · *click to begin again*

## Emotional throughline
Exile → one person who loved her → the deal that saved and cursed her →
fury as a way of living → stillness as sacrifice → **escape** → and finally a
quiet field where being alive is enough. The music carries the rage; the film
ends the only way Kainé would allow: no speech, no tears, standing up.
