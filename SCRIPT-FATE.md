# FATE/STAY NIGHT — 16-BIT · SABER
### A pixel-art short film. One HTML file. ~118 seconds + hold.
**Music — two of the user's MIDIs, switched by scene:**
- **"EMIYA"** (8-bit arrangement, 148 BPM, ~45s): the battle scenes. This file
  carries its own percussion tracks on MIDI channel 9 — the engine maps them
  onto the noise drum kit, so the real drum pattern plays, not a synthetic grid.
- **"Pride of the King"** (80 BPM, ~97s): everything else — the summoning, the
  quiet days, and the whole long goodbye. On each hand-off the music dips for a
  breath, then the other song takes the room.

> A boy summons a king. A lifetime later, he finds her again.

---

## Palette
Fuyuki at night: deep blues and moon silver, the red of command seals, Saber's
blue-and-armor. Then the film warms by stages — Camlann's bronze sunset, and at
the end Avalon: green-gold, wind, and nothing else. The gold of Excalibur and
the gold of Avalon's grass are the same color on purpose.

## Scenes

### 0 · TITLE — *silence*
A dark shed. A faint circle glows on the floorboards.
**FATE/STAY NIGHT — 16-BIT · SABER** · *PRESS START*

### 1 · THE SUMMONING (14s) — *Pride of the King, quiet*
Night. Moonlight through the shed door. The circle flares (a slow bloom, not a
flash) — and a knight in blue is kneeling in the light, invisible sword in hand.
> SABER: "Are you my Master?"

### 2 · THE DAYS BETWEEN (14s) — *Pride, warm*
The Emiya house at evening: engawa, warm shoji glow. Saber, sitting perfectly
straight, finishing her third bowl of rice. Shirou watches, sleeves rolled.
> SABER: "...Seconds, please."
> *The King of Knights took dinner very seriously.*

### 3 · THE WAR (24s) — ***EMIYA.*** *Drums from the file itself.*
Night city. Saber against a mountain of a man — Berserker, stone blade taller
than she is. Clash after clash, gold sparks, knockback, charge again.
Shirou shouting from the edge of the frame.
> *Thirteen times she struck. The giant laughed. She struck again.*

### 4 · EXCALIBUR (18s) — *EMIYA, peak*
Ryuudou temple. Above it, the Grail — a black swirl eating the sky.
Saber plants her feet. The invisible wind falls away, and the blade is gold.
Light gathers like rising rain, then a single golden pillar splits the dark.
> SABER: "EX — CALIBUR."

### 5 · FAREWELL (18s) — *Pride of the King returns, alone*
Dawn over the ruined temple. Saber is already becoming light — gold motes
streaming upward off her armor. Shirou runs. She smiles first.
> SABER: "Shirou — I love you."
Then the light finishes its work, and the sword-glint fades from the air.

### 6 · CAMLANN (14s) — *Pride, muffled and low*
A bronze hill of planted swords and torn banners. A young king lies beneath
the sunset, eyes closing — done, at last, with the battlefield.
> *The King of Knights closed her eyes...*
> *...and dreamed of a garden.*

### 7 · A LIFETIME (16s) — *Pride, steady*
One scene, four seasons scrolling past behind a walking figure: city, ruin,
desert, snow. Shirou, older each phase, stride unchanged — and phase by phase
he becomes the silhouette every Fate fan recognizes: the hair goes white, the
coat goes red, a shroud-tail trailing behind him in the wind.
> *He lived an entire life walking toward her.*
> *Until his hair turned white, and his coat turned red.*
> *A promise is a promise.*
(In Avalon he is the auburn-haired boy again — that is what arriving means.)

### 8 · AVALON (hold) — *Pride, warm, far away*
Green-gold grass to the horizon, wind moving through it in waves, one great
tree. A woman in white waits beneath it, hair down. From the left edge of
forever, a boy arrives — young again, because time means nothing here.
She turns. He stops. Neither hurries now; they have literally forever.
> SABER: "I told you. I would wait for you."

## Emotional throughline
Summon → belong → fight → sacrifice → separation (twice: she leaves him, then
she dies her own death centuries in the past) → a lifetime of walking → arrival.
The two musics ARE the two halves: EMIYA is the sound of striving; Pride of the
King is the sound of what the striving was for.
