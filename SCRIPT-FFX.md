# FINAL FANTASY X — 16-BIT
### A pixel-art short film. One HTML file. ~2 minutes + hold.
**Music:** the user's "To Zanarkand" MIDI (solo piano, 95 BPM, ~2:35), embedded and
rendered through the chiptune engine — triangle low register, vibrato square lead.
No drums, ever. The piece is a lullaby; the film moves at its pace.

> "Listen to my story."

---

## Palette
NieR's film was ash and bone. Spira is water and light: deep sea blues, teal pyreflies,
warm firelight, one dusty gold sunset. The two films should feel like different worlds
on the same shelf.

## Scenes

### 0 · TITLE — *silence*
A night sea. Stars. Moonlight path on the water.
**FINAL FANTASY X — 16-BIT** · *PRESS START*

### 1 · CAMPFIRE (14s) — *music enters, sparse and far away*
Ruins at night. Seven silhouettes around a small flickering fire.
> *Listen to my story. This... may be our last chance.*

### 2 · ZANARKAND (20s) — *verse*
The city of a thousand lights across black water — towers of lit windows, the
blitzball dome glowing, reflections shivering in the sea. A boy on a pier, watching.
Caption: `ZANARKAND — 1000 YEARS AGO`

### 3 · PILGRIMAGE (20s) — *verse, warmer*
Sunset side-scroll along the highroad: green hills, broken stone arches drifting by.
A summoner leads her guardians in single file — Yuna, Tidus, Auron, Lulu, Wakka,
Kimahri. A chocobo crosses the other way.
> YUNA: "I've learned how to smile... even when I'm feeling sad."

### 4 · THE SENDING (24s) — *the lead shimmers an octave above*
Night. A village burns far off. Yuna stands ON the water, staff in hand.
Teal rings spread slowly from her feet; pyreflies spiral up in streams
and rise until the sky is full of them.
> LULU: "The dead need guidance."

### 5 · SIN (12s) — *the music darkens under a lowpass veil*
A storm-grey sea. Something colossal surfaces on the horizon — a black hill
that shouldn't move, and does. The party stands very small on the cliff.
> AURON: "That... is Sin."

### 6 · FAREWELL (26s) — *quiet, heavy echo*
Zanarkand ruins at dusk. Pyreflies everywhere. Tidus begins to scatter —
pixel by pixel he comes apart into rising light. Yuna runs to hold him
and passes through.
> YUNA: "Stay with me until the end."
> TIDUS: "Not until the end... always."

### 7 · DAWN SEA (hold) — *the piano keeps playing, softly, to the end*
A cliff over a dawn ocean. Yuna alone, facing the water. She whistles —
the way he taught her. Pyreflies drift out to sea.
> "Never forget them."
`— THIS IS MY STORY —` · *click to begin again*

## Emotional throughline
Memory → wonder → companionship → grief made beautiful (the Sending) →
dread → letting go → carrying on. Where the NieR film earns its dawn by fighting
for it, this one earns it by accepting loss — the whistle answered by nothing,
and walking forward anyway.
